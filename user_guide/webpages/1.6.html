Configure Table Output to Map to our Schema

  1. We have preconfigured each wrapper template to connect to our database
  2. All you have to do is map your fields to the fields in our target schema using the Table Output step
  3. Double-click on the Table Output step.
  4. The configuration window belonging to this kind of step will appear.
  5. Go to Database Fields tab, click on Get fields to pull all of the fields from your previous steps.
  6. The fields on the right are the fields created from the previous steps in your transformation.
  7. The steps on the left are all of the fields in our target schema.
  8. If not all of the fields on the left exist, create them and do either:
    A. map fields from previous steps to them or
    B. add field/value pairs to the Add Constants Step
    (note these constant values apply to every row of your output (i.e. your entire dataset)
  9. When you are done, your Table Output step should look something like this,
    but mappings that make sense for your dataset:

  10. If you can narrow down location to Country, Country Sub-Division, Locality you can additionally map your location field from your previous steps to the Country, CountrySubDiv, and/or Locality respectively in our target schema.
  11. Click OK to finish defining the Table Output step.
