Welcome to Help Center!

The help center will help you generate and upload a wrapper for your dataset.

Your dataset must be Internet-accessible in order for you to contribute it.

Please note that in the following tutorials, the word transformation is synonymous with wrapper.

We have three tutorials to help you to get started submitting wrappers for your datasets:

    System Requirements: links to download java and our wrapper software
    Create New Wrappers: a guide to creating wrappers
    Register your Wrappers: a guide to register your wrapper on our site
